Peanut Butter And Jelly

A classic for kids and adults alike! This easy to make recipe consists of just three ingredients that you may have in your kitchen right now.

PBnJ's have been around for a long time, and there's no signs of that stopping. The sweet tart jelly combined with the salty and smooth peanut butter is a match made in heaven, but you don't have to die to try this.

Ingredients
  • Bread of your choice
  • Grape jelly
  • Peanut butter
Steps
  1. Place two slices of bread on a plate.
  2. Slather a generous amount of jelly on one slice of bread.
  3. Spread a good amount of peanut butter on the other slice of bread and combine the two pieces.
  4. Cut sandwich diagonally and enjoy!
